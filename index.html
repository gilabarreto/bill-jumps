About

Bill Jumps is a re-creation of a popular game that is found within Chrome's offline mode, but instead of featuring a dinosaur as the main character, it features Bill, a character who jumps over chairs in a manner reminiscent of his classic 90s video. To begin playing the game, the player simply needs to press the space key and they will be able to control Bill as he jumps over chairs. The game is designed to be enjoyable and entertaining, and the player will undoubtedly have a good time playing it.

The game was re-created and developed by Victor Gila Barreto, a Full Stack Web Developer and enthusiast of retro games. If you are interested in learning more about the author and his other projects, please click here for more information.
